üì¶ Drag & Drop Mod Installer

What it does: Installs mods, builds, Sims, or saves to the correct folder automatically.

  • Drag ZIP files or loose files directly onto the installer window.
  • Files are automatically:
    • Extracted (if zipped)

  • Works on its own or alongside Auto Mods.
  • Perfect for bulk downloads ‚Äî any included CC is sorted instantly.

üë§ Mod Profile Manager

What it does: Lets you save and switch between different Mods setups.

Your original Mods are always backed up. The first backup can never be overwritten.

  • Use profiles for different playstyles (for example: Vanilla, Build Mode, or Full Mods).
  • Great for testing new CC or playing with minimal mods during updates.

How to use:

  1. Arrange your /Mods folder how you want.
  2. Click Create New Profile, name it, and save.
  3. To switch profiles, select one and click Load Profile.
  4. If new mods are detected, choose Import New Mods or Replace Profile.

You can always restore your original Mods via More ‚û° View Backups.

üóÇÔ∏è Backup Sims 4 Files

What it does: Creates a zipped backup of your Mods, Saves, and/or Tray folders.

Why use it: Protects your game before organizing mods, testing CC, or updating.

How to use:

  1. Open More Tools ‚û° Settings & Utilities ‚û° Backup Files.
  2. Select what you want to back up (Mods, Saves, Tray).
  3. Choose a backup location with enough free space.
  4. Click OK.

To restore, unzip the backup and copy the contents back into your Sims 4 folders.

You can create as many backups as you like.
