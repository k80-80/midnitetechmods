‚ú® Getting Started

On first launch, Clean Launcher can create an Auto Mods folder inside your /Mods directory. Anything placed inside, from zips to .ts4scripts to Tray files, will be automatically extracted and sorted while the app runs in the system tray.

For easy drag-and-drop access, you can pin Auto Mods to Windows Quick Access. Files stay there, ready to test or organise.

To remove Auto Mods, disable it in Settings or from the tray icon. Deleting the folder manually will cause it to be recreated.
